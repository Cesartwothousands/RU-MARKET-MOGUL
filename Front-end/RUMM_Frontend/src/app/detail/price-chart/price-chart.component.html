Line Chart with Bollinger Bands

This line chart features Bollinger Bands, a technical analysis tool used in financial markets. It displays price movements along with the upper and lower bands, providing insights into market:

  • Real Time Price: Auccurate and complete realtime price data.
  • SMA Line: The Simple Moving Average (SMA) of the price over a specified period.
  • Upper Band: The center line plus a specified number of standard deviations.
  • Lower Band: The center line minus a specified number of standard deviations.
